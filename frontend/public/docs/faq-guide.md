# FMS — Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Welcome to the FMS AI Guide! Simple, direct answers for every role.

---

## 👤 General & Account

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
**A:** On the login screen, click **Forgot Password**. You'll receive a 6-digit OTP to your email (valid for 2 minutes). Enter it to set a new password.

**Q: Where can I change my profile settings?**
**A:** Click your name or avatar in the **top right corner** and select **Profile**.

**Q: Where can I find my profile?**
**A:** Click your name or avatar in the **top right corner** and select **Profile**.

**Q: How do I update my name or email?**
**A:** Click your name or avatar in the **top right corner**, select **Profile**, and update your details there.

**Q: My dashboard is empty, what do I do?**
**A:** Your account may not have a role assigned yet. Contact your company Admin to ensure your **Platform Role** is correctly set.

**Q: I was logged out unexpectedly, what happened?**
**A:** Your session expired. Simply log in again. If it keeps happening, contact your Admin.

**Q: Why did my role change not take effect?**
**A:** Log out and log back in to refresh your session with the updated role.

**Q: How do I log out?**
**A:** Click your name or avatar in the **top right corner** and select **Logout**.

**Q: What are the different roles in FMS?**
**A:** FMS has 4 roles: **Member** (executes tasks), **Controller** (manages project instances), **Admin** (manages users and templates), and **Superadmin** (manages all companies).

**Q: What is a Platform Role?**
**A:** A Platform Role determines what sections of the system you can access (like Admin or Member).

**Q: What is a Workflow Role?**
**A:** A Workflow Role defines your specific job (like Designer or Copywriter). The system uses this to automatically assign you to the correct tasks.

**Q: How do I get notifications?**
**A:** Notifications appear automatically in the **bell icon** at the top right. You'll get a sound alert and badge when it's your turn to act on something.

**Q: Why am I not getting notifications?**
**A:** Check the **bell icon** in the top right. Notifications only appear when a task or event specifically involves you.

## 🛠️ Member (Team Member)

**Q: How do I submit a task?**
**A:** Go to **My Tasks**, click on the task, check off **all checklist items**, then click **Submit Task** at the bottom.

**Q: How do I complete a task?**
**A:** Go to **My Tasks**, click on the task, check off **all checklist items**, then click **Submit Task** at the bottom.

**Q: Why is the submit button not working?**
**A:** You must check off **every item** in the checklist before the Submit button activates.

**Q: Why is my task Locked?**
**A:** Tasks run in sequence. Your task is waiting for the previous step to be finished and approved first. It will unlock automatically when that happens.

**Q: What does Locked mean?**
**A:** Your task is waiting for the previous step to be completed by someone else. It will unlock automatically.

**Q: What happens if my task is rejected?**
**A:** The task comes back to your **In Progress** list with a comment from the reviewer explaining what to fix. Update your work and submit again.

**Q: What happens if my task is approved?**
**A:** You will receive a notification that your task has been approved.

**Q: My task was rejected, what do I do?**
**A:** Open the task from **My Tasks**. You'll find a rejection comment explaining what to fix. Make the changes and click **Submit Task** again.

**Q: Where can I see my reviews?**
**A:** Go to the **My Reviews** tab on your dashboard to see all tasks you need to review.

**Q: How do I review a task?**
**A:** Go to **My Reviews**, open the task, check the submitted work, and click **Approve** or **Reject**. If rejecting, add a comment explaining what needs to be fixed.

**Q: How do I approve a task?**
**A:** Go to **My Reviews**, open the task, and click the **Approve** button.

**Q: How do I reject a task?**
**A:** Go to **My Reviews**, open the task, click **Reject**, and write a comment explaining what needs to be corrected.

**Q: What is a checklist?**
**A:** A checklist is a list of required items inside a task. You must tick off every item before you can submit the task.

**Q: How do I tick off checklist items?**
**A:** Open the task from **My Tasks** and click the checkbox next to each item to mark it done.

**Q: Where can I see my deadlines?**
**A:** Your tasks show due dates in the **My Tasks** view. Overdue tasks are highlighted.

**Q: What is the status PENDING_APPROVAL?**
**A:** This means you've submitted the task and it's now waiting for a reviewer to approve it. You don't need to do anything—just wait.

**Q: What is IN_PROGRESS status?**
**A:** The task is unlocked and assigned to you. You can work on it, check off items, and submit it.

**Q: What is COMPLETED status?**
**A:** The task is fully done and approved. No further action needed from you on this one.

## 🎮 Controller (Manager)

**Q: How do I start a new project (instance)?**
**A:** Go to **Instances** > **Create Instance**. Select a template and client, assign users to tasks, and click **Create**.

**Q: How do I create an instance?**
**A:** Go to **Instances** > **Create Instance**. Select a template and client, assign users to tasks, and click **Create**.

**Q: How do I launch a new project?**
**A:** Go to **Instances** > **Create Instance**. Select a template and client, assign users to tasks, and click **Create**.

**Q: How do I pause or stop an instance?**
**A:** In the **Instances** list, click on your instance and select **Pause** in the top right. You can **Resume** it later.

**Q: How do I pause a project?**
**A:** In the **Instances** list, click on your instance and select **Pause** in the top right. You can **Resume** it later.

**Q: How do I resume a paused instance?**
**A:** In the **Instances** list, find the paused project and click **Resume** in the top right of the details panel.

**Q: How do I change the assigned user for a task?**
**A:** Open the instance details, click the **Settings/User** icon next to the task, and select a new person from the dropdown.

**Q: How do I reassign a task?**
**A:** Open the instance details, click the **Settings/User** icon next to the task, and select a new person from the dropdown.

**Q: How do I change an approver for a task?**
**A:** Open the instance details, click the settings icon for the task, and select **Change Approver** to pick a new reviewer.

**Q: How do I change the reviewer for a task?**
**A:** Open the instance details, click the settings icon for the task, and select **Change Approver** to pick a new reviewer.

**Q: How do I manually Unlock a task?**
**A:** Open the instance details, find the locked task, and click on settings icon and then click on **Manual Unlock** button to bypass the sequence.

**Q: How do I manually unlock a locked task?**
**A:** Open the instance details, find the locked task, and click on settings icon and then click on **Manual Unlock** button to skip the sequence.

**Q: How does scheduling an instance work?**
**A:** When a project is created with a future start date, it stays in **Scheduled** status. At midnight on that date, the system automatically starts it and notifies the first person.

**Q: How does the "Repeat" feature work?**
**A:** When a project is set to repeat (like Weekly or Monthly), the system will automatically create a fresh copy of it as soon as the last task of the current project is finished.

**Q: How do I view overdue tasks?**
**A:** Check the **Performance** or **SLA** widget on your dashboard to see all tasks that have missed their deadlines.

**Q: How do I see which tasks are overdue?**
**A:** Check the **Performance** or **SLA** widget on your dashboard to see all tasks that have missed their deadlines.

**Q: What is a Quick Task?**
**A:** Use the **⚡ Quick Task** button on the Instances page for simple, one-off requests that don't need a full project template.

**Q: How do I create a quick task?**
**A:** Click the **⚡ Quick Task** button on the Instances page. This is for simple ad-hoc work like "Design a banner."

**Q: How do I view instance details?**
**A:** In the **Instances** list, click the **eye icon** next to an instance to open its detail view.

**Q: How do I view a project details?**
**A:** In the **Instances** list, click the **eye icon** next to the project to open its full detail view.

**Q: How do I bypass a task?**
**A:** Open the instance details, find the task, click the settings icon, and select **Bypass**. This force-completes the task and unlocks the next one.

**Q: What is the difference between SCHEDULED and ONGOING?**
**A:** **Scheduled** means the instance is created but hasn't started yet (start date is in the future). **Ongoing** means it's actively running with tasks being worked on.

**Q: How do I extend a task deadline?**
**A:** Open the instance details, click the settings icon for the overdue task, and select **Extend SLA** to push back the due date.

**Q: How do I set up a recurring project?**
**A:** When creating an instance via **Create Instance**, select a **Repeat** interval (Weekly, Monthly, etc.). The system will automatically restart the project when the current one completes.

**Q: How do I view performance metrics?**
**A:** Go to the **Performance Metrics** section in your sidebar for a full breakdown of team performance and SLA compliance.

**Q: How do I add a manual task?**
**A:** Go to **Manual Tasks** in your sidebar and click **Add Task**. Manual tasks are one-off tasks not tied to any project instance.

**Q: Can I reopen a task that has already been completed?**
**A:** Yes. If a client requests changes after a task is marked `COMPLETED`, open the **Instance Details** modal, click the **Settings (⚙)** icon on that completed task, and choose **Unlock Task**. A "Client Revision Request" dialog will appear. Fill in the internal reason and optionally paste the client's feedback, then click **Reopen for Revision**. The task will restart from the beginning and all subsequent tasks will be locked automatically.

**Q: A client wants changes but the project is already completed. What do I do?**
**A:** You can reopen any completed task even after the whole instance is `COMPLETED`. Open the **Instance Details**, click ⚙ on the task the client wants changed, and choose **Unlock Task**. The system will automatically revert the instance to `ONGOING` and lock all tasks that came after the reopened one.

**Q: What happens to tasks after I reopen one for client revision?**
**A:** All tasks that come **after** the reopened task (i.e., higher task order) are immediately set to `LOCKED`. Their checklists and approval levels are reset. Once the reopened task is completed and approved again, the next task unlocks automatically — just like a fresh start from that step.

**Q: Will the member know their task was reopened by the client?**
**A:** Yes. They receive an in-app notification that says the task was reopened due to a client revision request. If you filled in the **Client's Feedback** field in the dialog, that message also appears inside the member's task view as a rejection comment.

**Q: Is reopening a task for revision logged anywhere?**
**A:** Yes. Every client revision action is permanently logged in the audit trail (stored in `task_bypass_logs`) with the action code `CLIENT_REVISION`, the controller who triggered it, the timestamp, and the reason given.

**Q: Can I reopen a task that is IN_PROGRESS or LOCKED?**
**A:** No. The "Unlock Task" option only appears on tasks with status `COMPLETED`. For `LOCKED` tasks, use **Manual Unlock** instead.

## ⚖️ Admin (Company Setup)

**Q: How do I add a new team member?**
**A:** Go to **Users** in the sidebar, click **Add User**, fill in their name and email, set their **Role**, and click **Save**.

**Q: How do I create a new user?**
**A:** Go to **Users** in the sidebar, click **Add User**, fill in their name and email, set their **Role**, and click **Save**.

**Q: How do I set a user's role?**
**A:** Go to **Users**, click on the user, and update their **Platform Role** (Member, Controller, Admin) and **Workflow Role** (Copywriter, Designer, etc.).

**Q: How do I change a user's role?**
**A:** Go to **Users**, click on the user, and update their **Platform Role** (Member, Controller, Admin) and **Workflow Role** (Copywriter, Designer, etc.).

**Q: How do I remove a user?**
**A:** Go to **Users**, find the user, and click the **Delete** or **Remove** option.

**Q: How do I create a new project template?**
**A:** Go to **Templates**, click **Create Template**, add a name and description, then add your steps (tasks) with roles and deadlines. Click **Create** to save.

**Q: How do I create a workflow?**
**A:** Go to **Templates**, click **Create Template**, add a name and description, then add your steps (tasks) with roles and deadlines. Click **Create** to save.

**Q: How do I add tasks to a template?**
**A:** After creating a template, open it and click **Add Task**. Set the task title, assigned role, estimated time, checklist items, and whether approval is required.

**Q: How do I set up approval for a task in a template?**
**A:** When adding a task to a template, enable **Approval Required** and set the number of approval levels (1 to 5).

**Q: How do I set company holidays?**
**A:** Go to **Settings** or the **Holidays** tab. Add your company's non-working days so they're excluded from task deadlines.

**Q: How do I add a holiday?**
**A:** Go to the **Holidays** tab, click **Add Holiday**, and enter the date and name.

**Q: How do I copy an existing template?**
**A:** Go to **Templates**, find the template you want to duplicate, and click the **Copy/Duplicate** button.

**Q: How do I delete a template?**
**A:** Go to **Templates**, find the template, and click the **Delete** option. Note: this only removes the blueprint, not any active projects.

**Q: How do I add a client?**
**A:** Go to the **Clients** tab and click **Add Client** to create a new client record.

**Q: How do I view all users?**
**A:** Go to the **Users** tab in your sidebar to see all team members in your company.

**Q: Why are tasks not being auto-assigned when I create an instance?**
**A:** Make sure users have the correct **Workflow Role** set (e.g., "copywriter", "designer"). The system matches tasks to users by their workflow role.

## 💡 Troubleshooting

**Q: The submit button isn't working!**
**A:** Make sure you've checked **every item** in the task checklist. The button only becomes active once all items are ticked.

**Q: Why can't I submit my task?**
**A:** All checklist items must be completed. Open the task and make sure every checkbox is ticked before submitting.

**Q: I didn't get a notification for a new task.**
**A:** Notifications only appear when it's specifically your turn. Check your **My Tasks** and **My Reviews** tabs directly to see if anything is waiting.

**Q: A task is stuck and won't move forward.**
**A:** This is a Controller action. The Controller can use **Manual Unlock** to bypass the stuck task and move the project forward.

**Q: The project isn't starting even though I created it.**
**A:** Check if the instance has a future start date — it will be in **Scheduled** status. The Controller can force-activate it immediately from the instance details.

**Q: How do I export a report?**
**A:** Go to the **Reports** section, select your date range, and click **Download CSV** or **Export**.

**Q: There is a banner saying "New Updates Available". What should I do?**
**A:** Click on the banner to refresh your view with the latest data. This happens when someone else updated something in the background.

**Q: I see a "New Updates Available" prompt.**
**A:** Click on it to soft-refresh the page. Your current work won't be lost — only the data list updates.

**Q: What is an instance?**
**A:** An instance is a live, active copy of a project template. Think of the template as a recipe and the instance as the actual dish being made for a specific client.

**Q: What is a template?**
**A:** A template is a reusable workflow blueprint. It defines the steps (tasks), roles, deadlines, and approvals — like a recipe you can run for any client.
